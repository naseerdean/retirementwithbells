Market Shock Scenarios ℹ️

How shocks work:
• A shock is a one‑off fall in that model year (e.g., −30% in Year 5).
• The fall applies to all capital wrappers (Pension, ISA, GIA, Offshore Bond); guaranteed incomes are unchanged.
• After the shock year, balances resume normal growth.
• Multiple shocks can be added and will stack if in the same year.
